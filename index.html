About Me

About Jonathan Aigbe Hey, I’m Jonathan Aigbe—a Nigerian artist who’s been creating for over a decade now. I work with soft chalk pastels, acrylics, and oils to tell stories rooted in African tradition. I studied Fine Art and graduated in 2017, and since then, my work has been all about celebrating the culture, beauty, and everyday life of my people.

My art is inspired by the world around me—nostalgic childhood memories, bold African women, historical moments, and the rich cultural rhythm of Nigeria. Whether it’s a simple market scene or a nod to ancient myths, I paint from a place of pride, identity, and love for home.

I'm open to commissions, collaborations, and building meaningful connections through art. Feel free to reach out.
